So I've finally decided to start blogging about things that i find interesting.
What exactly does that consist of? Finding a framework for a blogging site, and deciding what to blog
about. 

## First agenda create a blog
So I searched far / wide looking for suitable frameworks to use for blogging before stumbling upon
_**Jekyll**_. So you may be asking what makes _**Jekyll**_ so special? Well the answer is simple, it's easy
to use, fairly simple to setup, and creating post is a breeze. So you should probably change your question
to why wouldn't you use _**Jekyll**_? There's tons of articles on the internet about getting started with _**Jekyll**_
so i won't be providing a play by play.

## What am i going to be blogging about?
Well that's an easy question to answer. I'll be blogging about anything technological that i find interesting
whether it be some new framework, or some new handheld that's caught my eye. This will give you the opportunity
to find out what i'm interested in.
